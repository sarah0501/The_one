Lost Münchberg

Photo series of neglected buildings in Münchberg

Subject

Photo series of your hometown

Field

Photography

Date

11.2018 – 01.2019
first semester

About

Every city has its own atmosphere and we connect something with the location. During this work, we were asked to express a feeling, a mood that we connect with our current location using photos.
At the time of this work, I didn’t know Münchberg well since I just moved in. So, the first thing I did, was taking a long walk through the city. I looked at the buildings, the materials, the people. Münchberg has a lot of beautiful buildings, but it was the neglected, abandoned buildings that caught my attention. Therefore, I decided to photograph this kind of buildings. With this work I didn’t just want to show the bad condition of a lot of buildings in this city, my focal point was to find a way to make the motives look aesthetic and interesting.
