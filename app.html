Tripulator

Route calculation for public transportation

Subject

E-Scooters in public space

Field

UI/UX, Interaction Design

Date

12.2019 – 01.2020
third semester

About

Study project in collaboration with Brose Group.

E-Scooters became a new public mode of transport in a lot of larger cities.
For this project, we did a wide investigation about how they influence the traffic, public security and a lot more. We were asked to find a solution or advancement for one or more problems caused by them.
The starting point of my project was the question, in which situations the use of E-Scooters is appropriate, and in which other means of transport would be the better choice. To make the decision easier, I created a prototype for an app, which calculates the best public transportation for the desired route. The basis of this calculation are factors such as the weather, the distance, the environment, road surface, the interests of the user and more.
