Media Design (fourth semester)

Since I can remember, I was interested in creating new things, making my ideas visible. In school, I always was the girl who scribbled all of her notebooks with ideas I wanted to implement or simply some studies on how to display certain things. In primary school, I even won a small design competition. As I grew older, my interest in design became stronger, but I slowly turned away from painting and drawing and developed great favor for digital design. I´m especially enthusiastic about web-, print-, UX/UI- and logo design.

Freetime

The few hours that I don´t spend in front of my laptop, I´m out in nature climbing some mountains, riding through hills with my mountain bike or simply discovering nearby forests on a walk. Furthermore, I´m in love with listening to music and end the evening with a good series.

Personality

I love working in a team and developing awesome ideas together. I am very precise and considerate about my work as I have high demands on myself. Moreover, I´m a quite curious person, I love to try out new techniques and broaden my knowledge.
