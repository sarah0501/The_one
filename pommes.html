Wer Pommes sein will muss leiden

A short animated video about the pressure to externally adapt to society.

Subject

Collection

Field

Animation, film

Date

12.2019 – 01.2020
third semester

About

This project was about making a short animated movie that contains a collection and fits nicely to the music. I decided to deal with the topic of cosmetic surgery and the pressure to adapt externally to society. Inspired by Melanie Matinez´s song ‘’Mrs. Potato Head”, I chose potatoes as the main actors in my film.
Throughout the movie, the potatoes are processed into fries. The main character initially escapes these interventions, but as he notices that he isn´t accepted the way he is, he wants to endure them. Whether he pulls it through or changes his mind at the last moment should remain unclear due to the open end.
This movie was created as a mashup of a stop-motion movie and a normal film. The eyes of the potatoes were animated on the pictures afterward.
