Portrait of a potter

Subject

Portrait of a person in a profession

Field

Photography, print design

Date

12.2019 – 01.2020
third semester

About

What you can see on this page, is the result of the task to create a two to four-page long article for a fictional magazine. Therefore, we had to photograph a person who is in a profession or is engaged in volunteering. The nature of their work, their commitment should be visible in some form. A small explanatory text complements the pictures in the magazine.
For my work, I wanted to portrait a person with a job that is not that common anymore. After some days of research, I found a potter based in Thurnau that fit my expectations very well.
