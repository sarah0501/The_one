Im Überschwang

Study Project of designing a CD Booklet

Subject

CD Booklet for the Leipziger Barockorchester

Field

Print design

Date

12.2018 – 01.2019
first semester

About

As our final project of the first semester, we were asked to design a booklet for the new album of the “Leipziger Barockorchester”. This task fulfilled exercise purposes, we have not officially designed for the orchestra. The inside of the 8-page booklet had to be monochrome, the text and the picture of the orchestra were given to us.
