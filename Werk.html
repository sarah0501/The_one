Werkzeug Hand Buch

A little book about tools in the use of mediadesign students

Subject

Collection

Field

Editorial

Date

10.2019 – 11.2019
third semester

About

Everyone has something to collect. These can be carefully chosen things like coins, or more unconscious collections like the contents of a woman's bag
For this project, I was asked to represent such a collection of small, trivial things in a book.
I decided to represent a collection of tools that me and my fellow students use throughout our study. The focus is on analog tools, the digital ones are only roughly mentioned.
On the one hand, I set myself the goal to inform about the diversity of the different tools in your course. On the other hand, I wanted to display them in direct use to gain an insight into our atmosphere and the way we work.
As my target group, I define people who are interested in media design, as well as the students themselves that can use the brochure to get an overview of all the tools and maybe learn something about one or the other.
The result is a kind of manual in a handy format that can be taken with you at any time and requires minimal space.

On behalf of my editorial professor, this book will be printed in a small edition to be given as a gift to future media design students.
