Subject

Collection

Field

third semester

About

This website was built to tell the story of packages that my mother got when she was younger. She grew up in a small village in Silesia (Poland). At this time, there was an extreme food shortage in this region. For this reason, packages with food, toys, etc. were sent to children in these villages by private individuals or organizations from Germany. These packages meant a lot to my mother and her siblings. They represent small insights into the modern western world.
To tell our story, we had to use a page builder tool that was affordable. Most of us chose pageflow, because of the wide range of available functions and easy use.
